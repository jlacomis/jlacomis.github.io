About Me

I am a Ph. D. student in the Software and Societal Systems Department (formerly the Institute for Software Research) at Carnegie Mellon University. I am a member of two research groups: STRUDEL, with my co-advisor Bogdan Vasilescu and squaresLab, with my co-advisor Claire Le Goues. I am interested in maintenance, debugging, and reverse-engineering of software.

I have a B.A., Computer Science from the University of Virginia and an A.S., Computer Science from Piedmont Virginia Community College. Before grad school I had various jobs in fields as diverse as restaurant management, book binding, and MRI service.
